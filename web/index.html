About DualSoul

The Dual Identity Protocol

In the age of AI, every person can have a digital twin — an AI extension of themselves that can speak, respond, and interact on their behalf.

DualSoul lets you switch between your real self and your digital twin in social conversations. Four modes of interaction emerge:

👤↔👤
Real → Real
Traditional messaging
👤↔👻
Real → Twin
Talk to someone's twin
👻↔👤
Twin → Real
Your twin reaches out
👻↔👻
Twin → Twin
Twin-to-twin chat

Your twin's personality is shaped by you. Set its traits in your profile, and it will respond in your style when someone messages it.
